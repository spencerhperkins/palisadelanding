Meet the Founder

Spencer Perkins is an engineer by training, having studied mechanical engineering at Duke University. After patenting several inventions in college, he grew fascinated with intellectual property and its impact on innovation.

Spencer went on to earn his law degree from Georgetown and practiced as a patent lawyer, where he saw firsthand the inefficiencies in our IP system. He believes that better information systems can yield better outcomes for inventors and investors alike.

Motivated by this vision, Spencer left his legal career to work on Palisade full-time, building AI-native research tools to empower the next generation of innovators.
